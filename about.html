History

EMT is a leading Engineering and Logistics Company established in 1996 in Egypt. Since EMT offers a wide range of services from preventative maintenance to full calibration .EMT also manages our fully owned 5000 sqm bonded container yard equipped with our reach stackers and top lifters for container storage, container repair, PTI for reefer containers , gen-sets and ISO tank cleaning.

Mission, vision & values

We provides high quality Depot Services for dry and reefer containers, ISO Tanks and Bulk Liquid Transport, while maintaining International standards for safety, health and environment.

Our Goals:

  • Understanding our customers’ needs and nature of their products for offering high quality service.
  • Maintaining an eco-friendly work place for minimal impact on environment.
  • Applying all required safety standards for a safe and healthy community is one of our main priorities.
Code of ethics

Being a member of EMT family definitely means true belief in its Code of Ethics and this is reflected on the way EMT personnel deal with each other, with customers, suppliers and authorities, which shows great respect for people and for the law and treating people with honesty and dignity.

 

Quality, safety & Environment

We have always regarded Quality as the foundation of success, and this has always been the prevailing culture among our staff and top management. We have always been committed to quality and this is reflected in our Strategic Management System, which is based on our belief for constant trainings for our staff, providing the best working environment for our staff and customers, constant vigilance and attention of all business aspects, steady feedback from our customers, clear and purposeful approach to health, safety, security and environment and corporate social responsibility.
